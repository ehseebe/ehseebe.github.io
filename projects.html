Vagary

Vagary is a wandering journey; a whimsical, wild or unusual idea, desire, or action.

An app for lovers of travel who are itching to explore the world. Immerse yourself in the experience of discovering the sights and sounds of a new city from the safety of your home.

Teacherest

Like Pinterest, but for educators. Teacherest is a multi-page app with single page app features. Search through all resources by category or keyword and click on a resource to view it on a new page. Users can favourite or join the conversation and comment.

Node.js, Express, PostgresSQL, JS, jQuery and Ajax, EJS/CSS

Scheduler

Happy scheduling!

React.js using Axios, Classnames and SASS, Ruby on Rails, and tested with StoryBook, Jest and Cypress
